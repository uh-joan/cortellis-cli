Blind Spots the Council Caught (in this Verification Round)

Construct Validity of CPI

Nobody asked whether the 5 factors are the RIGHT 5. A score built on wrong factors launders opinion as math. Scenario analysis inherits garbage inputs.

Weights Should Be Learned, Not Legislated

20/30/20/15/15 is a committee compromise dressed as a model. Historical regression on deal outcomes or launch success would produce defensible weights in a weekend.

Schema Contract for Downstream Consumers

Cross-skill integration was dropped without even publishing the output schema other skills would consume. This is why integration "wasn't implemented" — there was nothing to integrate against.

The Counterfactual Question
